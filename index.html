Soul Narrative

In a noisy world 'expert' voices are not always reliable

But there are people with an intelligent heart who apply knowledge wisely and share it.

In this spirit we probe to learn and get a footing on matters that have an impact on our lives.

The aim of this site is to soulfully interact, speak the truth, and exchange information regarding world developments, in a dynamic present/past/future tense context. Topics of interest may include immunizations, NATO, Julian Assange, the military industrial complex, Russiagate, etc.

Forms of information and exchange may range from articles and interviews, podcasts and videos, quick takes and clickable charts to somehow simplify subjects make them fun, and generate clarity.
